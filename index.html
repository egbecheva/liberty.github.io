Truly Decentralized Digital Currency Marketplace

When using the Liberty DEX (decentralized exchange), trades are made between peers, brokered by servicenodes and completed quickly. Coins remain in your personal wallet until transactions are executed and new coins can be added at any time by servicenode owners.

Be a part from Liberty Network

Running a Servicenode is easy & profitable! You will receive a fee for verifying the transactions on the network.

Liberty Roadmap

Initial release
June, 2018
  1. Website published
  2. Wallets released
  3. Decentralized Exchange activated
  4. Several coins added to the DEX
  5. Demo and setup videos published
  6. Config tool created
Brand Awareness Campaign
July, 2018
  1. Masternodes Online listing
  2. CryptoBridge listing
  3. Social media engagement
  4. Recruitment of additional team members
  5. More coins added to the DEX
Whitepaper, New Platform Dev
April, 2018
  1. CoinMarketCap listing
  2. Whitepaper publication
  3. Onboarding new team members
  4. Begin research and development for the LEAP platform
Brand Growth, Strategic Partnerships
Q3-Q4, 2018
  1. Pursue strategic partnerships, particulary those that facilitate delivery on the fiat to crypto goals.
  2. Continued brand growth, awareness and education efforts
  3. Create proposal portal for funding community- based projects
  4. Create tooling to ease any barriers to entry for traders on the DEX
Fiat to Crypto Development
Q1-Q2, 2019
  1. Development of mobile apps for fiat to crypto purchases and trading on Liberty DEX
  2. Proposal portal available for mainstream use
